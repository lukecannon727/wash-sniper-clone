FAQ

  • Do I need to keep the browser alive for the sniper to work? Yes. You should also check your device / browser settings to ensure the tab doesn't fall asleep if you leave it open. In my experience, as long as it's focused, it'll work
  • Do I need $WASH to use the sniper? No, there is a free tier, but it'll be vastly outperformed by other snipers (or maybe even your bare hands), so you'll either be very unlikely to buy or have a high entry. Even so, this could still be alright for sniping $HYPE
  • Will the $WASH requirements change in future? Could. Won't do it without talking with users first
  • Why can't I just open multiple sniper windows (on multiple devices)? You can. If you're logged in with the same wallet holding $WASH, this is unlikely to improve anything due to reused nonces. Worse, it might block some tx from succeeding due to L1 nonce ordering requirements. However, if you login with different wallets holding $WASH, this problem won't occur. On this topic - will add in feature to easier accomodate multiple logins on the same browser in future
  • I'll add more when I think of / get asked them
